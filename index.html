Hospitals maintain float pools and on-call staff not as a luxury — but as basic operational responsibility. When a nurse calls out, the system activates. The patient never knows there was a problem. That infrastructure is considered the minimum standard of serious care delivery. Most private home care doesn't work this way. One caregiver, no backup, and when they don't show — the family absorbs the gap entirely. We built our concierge model around the same coverage logic that serious care institutions have always used. Your home deserves that standard too.
