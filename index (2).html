Our Story

Built from lived experience.

The name Discover Green Care is not a marketing choice. It is the name of the street where we first cared for someone we loved — where Kinsey's family spent years supporting her father through progressive decline, learning everything about what good care requires and what its absence costs.

"Kinsey had prayed her father would walk her down the aisle — long before she and Denzel were even dating. He did. He collapsed shortly after the ceremony, unbeknownst to her in that moment of joy. He was gone two months later, on January 13, 2021."

Denzel stepped into that caregiving season seamlessly — not because he was asked, but because it was already in him. Long before they met, he had spent years as a private caregiver in family homes, building the kind of intuitive, dignified care presence that cannot be taught in a classroom.

Together, they built the service they wish had existed. Not a staffing agency. Not a corporate franchise. A concierge care practice operated by two people who understand — from the inside — what it costs a family to show up for someone they love, and what it means when reliable care finally arrives.

Our Difference

What makes us different is operational, not promotional.

Every agency says they're reliable. We built the infrastructure that makes reliability a system, not a promise.

01
Backup Coverage Built Into Concierge

For clients on our Continuity Care tier, we maintain an on-call caregiver structure specifically so that when your primary caregiver has an emergency, we handle it — not you. You receive a notification that coverage is arranged, not a problem to solve. This is what the concierge retainer makes possible.

02
Defined Responsibility at Every Tier

We charge more for more responsibility — not just more time. Our tiered model means you always know exactly what we're accountable for and what falls within our scope.

03
Structured Medical Appointment Support

Before every medical visit, we collect concerns and questions from both the client and family — along with their communication preferences for how much assistance they want during the appointment itself. Our caregiver attends with a documentation template, captures what was discussed, and delivers a structured follow-up report to all authorized parties. Families who can't be in the room always know exactly what happened in it.

04
Communication That Travels

Our concierge clients receive regular written care summaries. Families in other states stay informed — not because they have to ask, but because we build it into the service.

05
Intentionally Small by Design

We deliberately limit the number of families we serve. When you're a client of Discover Green Care, you receive our full attention — not a fraction of it.

The Standard We Hold Ourselves To

Hospitals don't leave gaps in coverage to chance. Neither do we.

Hospitals maintain float pools and on-call staff not as a luxury — but as basic operational responsibility. When a nurse calls out, the system activates. The patient never knows there was a problem. That infrastructure is considered the minimum standard of serious care delivery.

Most private home care doesn't work this way. One caregiver, no backup, and when they don't show — the family absorbs the gap entirely. We built our concierge model around the same coverage logic that serious care institutions have always used. Your home deserves that standard too.

  • On-call backup caregiver roster maintained continuously
  • After-hours escalation line for concierge clients
  • Documented coverage protocols, not verbal promises
  • Active ownership — we manage disruptions so you don't have to
